What is this?

We are getting married! Turns out weddings are a great excuse to throw really fun parties. That's all this is; it's not a reception, but rather a chance to come have a lot of fun with a lot of fun people.

What should I wear?

Anything that will facilitate game and whimsy; e.g. your favorite t-shirt, a hawaiian button-down, or an American flag fanny pack.

So, where exactly is this?

The Underground Social Hall at Provo Town Square. Look for stairs that go underground (appropriately). If parking on Center Street isn't working out, you could park in unmarked stalls in the parking garage on 100 W and enter through the stairs in the back. I'm totally winging those directions, but I think they're right. Or at least close enough.

What can I expect from this party?

Dancing, socializing, and some light refreshments. What not to expect: formal attire, long lines of people, rare exotic birds.

Do I need to bring anything?

Bring yourself and a good attitude, just like you learned in little league.
